## Modes of Vim editor

1.**Command Mode**

2.**Insert Mode**

3.**Execution Mode**

4.**Visual Mode**

## Insert Mode:

**insert**
````
i
````
**new line below**
````
o
````
**new line above**
````
O
````
## Execution mode:
**save**
````
w
````
**quit**
````
q
````
**save and exit**
````
x or wq
````
