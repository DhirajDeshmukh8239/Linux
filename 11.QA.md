## Question 1:
** explain each term of `dhiraj@Dhiraj:~$`?**

**Answer:** 
- dhiraj -username
- @ -separate username and hostname
- Dhiraj -hostname
- : -separate user/host from current working directory
- ~ -pwd
- $ -means you are a regular (non-root) user.
